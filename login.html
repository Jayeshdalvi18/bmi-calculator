About BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator is a tool to help you determine your body mass index (BMI) based on your height and weight. It's a great way to understand your overall health and fitness level. The result is categorized into underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obesity, according to standard health guidelines.

Features

  • Choose Metric or Imperial units.
  • Instantly calculate your BMI results.
  • Receive health advice from BMI
  • Contact expert nutritionists for guidance.
